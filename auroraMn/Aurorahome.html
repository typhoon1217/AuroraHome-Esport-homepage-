Our Mission

Aurora Esports is a community run organization that strives to create a fun and inclusive environment for players of all ranks of Overwatch. Our staff has years of experience creating the best possible experience for our members. We run three seasons of our auction league style tournament format, Aurora Overwatch Clash, annually and are now looking to expand to monthly pre-made team tournaments in the near future. Watch this short video to get a better understanding of what you can expect from the Aurora Esports community. From Bronze 5 to Champion 1 all players will have a place they feel welcomed and belong. Whether you're a player new to competitive Overwatch or someone just wanting to make new friends and join a great community, join us today!
